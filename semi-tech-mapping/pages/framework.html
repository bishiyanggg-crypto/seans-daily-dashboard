Variant Perception / Key Debates

把行业地图进一步翻译成共识、非共识、受益链条和证伪条件。

AI capex 是长期平台迁移，还是被短期算力稀缺和融资环境放大的周期？

Consensus
AI infrastructure demand remains structurally strong and supply-constrained.
Variant
如果训练 scaling law 或 enterprise ROI 放缓，GPU/HBM/optical/ODM 的订单可见度会先在哪一层破？
Winners
NVIDIA, Broadcom, AMD, Marvell, TSMC, SK hynix, Micron, Vertiv, Delta, Arista, Innolight, Quanta, Wiwynn.
Risks
High-multiple AI supply-chain names with customer concentration, pass-through margin risk or late-cycle capacity additions.
Falsifier
Order pushouts across ODM/optical/substrate layers while GPU supply improves; cloud capex cuts without offsetting utilization data.

Custom ASIC 会不会显著削弱 GPU 经济池？

Consensus
GPU remains dominant for general-purpose training/inference, while custom ASIC expands total AI silicon TAM.
Variant
Hyperscaler workloads是否足够标准化，使ASIC在推理或内部训练中获得更大份额？
Winners
Broadcom, Marvell, Alchip, GUC, TSMC, Synopsys, Cadence, Astera/Credo for connectivity.
Risks
GPU-only exposure if software ecosystem and time-to-market advantages narrow.
Falsifier
ASIC ramps fail to reach volume or remain limited to narrow workloads; GPU software attach and inference share continue rising.

HBM 是 memory 新结构性利润池，还是高景气 DRAM 周期的另一种表现？

Consensus
HBM mix structurally improves memory margins and tightens commodity DRAM supply.
Variant
HBM capacity expansion会不会在 12-24 个月后形成新一轮供给过剩？
Winners
SK hynix, Micron, Samsung, Advantest, Hanmi Semiconductor, Lam, Applied Materials, DISCO, BESI.
Risks
Memory names if investors annualize peak ASP/margins; late entrants if qualification misses.
Falsifier
HBM pricing rolls over while capacity additions accelerate; commodity DRAM supply discipline breaks.

先进封装/CoWoS 是独立利润池，还是 TSMC captive advantage 的延伸？

Consensus
Advanced packaging remains a bottleneck and captures more value in AI systems.
Variant
OSAT和设备/材料供应商能拿到多少经济价值，还是主要被foundry和GPU平台吸收？
Winners
TSMC, ASE, Amkor, BESI, ASMPT, Hanmi, Unimicron, Ibiden, Kinsus, TOWA, Technoprobe.
Risks
Traditional OSAT capacity with limited advanced-package qualification.
Falsifier
Advanced packaging capacity normalizes faster than expected; pricing or utilization weakens despite AI demand.

中国半导体国产替代是可持续份额提升，还是政策驱动的局部过热？

Consensus
Localization remains a multi-year structural theme across equipment, materials, analog/RF/power and mature foundry.
Variant
哪些环节真正形成技术/客户壁垒，哪些只是受益于补贴、进口替代和订单前置？
Winners
NAURA, AMEC, Piotech, Anji, SMIC, Hua Hong, Will Semi, Maxscend, SG Micro, StarPower, JCET, Shennan, Innolight.
Risks
Global suppliers with high China mix; China names exposed to price competition and subsidy-led overcapacity.
Falsifier
Localization revenue grows but margins collapse; major tools/materials fail advanced-node qualification.

WFE 是否进入 AI/HBM 驱动的新上行周期？

Consensus
AI, HBM and leading-edge logic raise equipment intensity and extend WFE upcycle.
Variant
当前订单中有多少是真实 multi-year demand，多少是 China pull-forward 或 memory catch-up？
Winners
ASML, AMAT, Lam, KLA, TEL, ASM International, Advantest, DISCO, Screen, Onto, Nova.
Risks
Equipment names if China/export-control pull-forward reverses or memory capex pauses.
Falsifier
Backlog converts poorly, orders roll over despite AI demand, or customers cut capex after pull-forward.

Optical / 800G / 1.6T 是长期AI网络瓶颈，还是模块价格快速商品化？

Consensus
AI scale-out requires faster optical links and supports 800G/1.6T growth.
Variant
价值会留在DSP/laser/component，还是被模块组装和客户压价稀释？
Winners
Innolight, Eoptolink, Coherent, Lumentum, Fabrinet, Marvell, MACOM, Credo, Broadcom.
Risks
Module assemblers if ASP erosion outruns unit growth; telecom-exposed names if cloud strength masks carrier weakness.
Falsifier
Unit growth persists but revenue/margin underperform because pricing falls faster than expected.

AI value是否向 power / cooling / connectors 等 industrial layers 外溢？

Consensus
Data-center power density and rack-scale architecture raise non-chip content.
Variant
这些工业链条是结构性长期受益，还是订单高峰后的项目型周期？
Winners
Vertiv, Eaton, Schneider, Delta, Amphenol, TE Connectivity, Luxshare, Lotes, BizLink, Auras, Lite-On.
Risks
Names with project-margin dilution, customer concentration or capacity built for temporary bottlenecks.
Falsifier
Backlog grows but margins fall; project timing slips while chip supply normalizes.

汽车/工业半导体去库存何时真正结束？

Consensus
Auto/industrial is bottoming after a long inventory correction, but recovery timing varies by product.
Variant
哪些公司是真正 end-demand recovery，哪些只是 channel restock？
Winners
NXP, Infineon, STMicro, Renesas, TI, ADI, ON Semi, MPS, ROHM, Melexis, X-FAB, Tower, Murata, Yageo.
Risks
Auto/industrial names if distributors rebuild too early or EV growth slows further.
Falsifier
Orders recover without sell-through; utilization remains weak and pricing pressure persists.
