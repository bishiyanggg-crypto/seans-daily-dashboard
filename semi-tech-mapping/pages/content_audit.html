Audit Rules

每类字段对应不同证据标准，审计时不能用低层级来源覆盖高层级来源。

Field groupControlling sourceEvidence label
Company identity / ticker / exchangeCIQ security master; exchange listing page; company IRverified_fact only after ticker/exchange matched
Business description / productsLatest annual report, 10-K/20-F, company IR profile, investor presentationreported_fact until tied to current filing/IR
Segment assignmentCompany filings + product revenue mix + analyst classificationinference until reviewer approves classification
Priority Core/Important/WatchlistAnalyst judgment backed by liquidity, relevance, market cap, source availabilityassumption / analyst judgment, not company fact
Market cap / revenue / margin / multiples / consensusCIQ export with as-of date and field definitionsstale/unknown until refreshed
Cycle/KPI/playbook textPrimary industry sources + company commentary + CIQ revisionsdiligence-grade framework, not verified company fact
